About

Michelle Beach

Owner & Lead Bookkeeper

In addition to being a bookkeeper, I’m a cultural anthropologist (M.A. from University of Colorado) and aspiring herbalist. I’ve always been analytical, and as a child I remember spending hours in my room, creating lists and calendars for goals I wanted to achieve and coming up with different systems to organize my stuff. In college, I had a notebook in which I wrote down every dime I spent and categorized my expenses at the end of each month, constantly reworking my budget. I’m not as obsessive about every personal expense anymore, but I still get a thrill out of coming up with a new budget to reach my financial goals. I’m also passionate about helping others and making the world a better place (hence the anthropology degree). Doing bookkeeping for wellness professionals allows me to combine my natural talents with my desire to help others and support businesses that I truly believe in.

I’m certified in Xero, QuickBooks Online, and always learning more.
